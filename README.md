# Mood Social

> A privacy-first social media platform where connections are built around shared emotions, not permanent profiles.

## About

Mood Social is a social networking platform designed to encourage authentic, meaningful interactions while protecting user privacy. Instead of maintaining a permanent online presence, users express how they feel in the moment and connect with others experiencing similar emotions.

Every time a user logs in, they are asked to:

* Select their current mood.
* Share a brief reason for that mood.

The platform then curates a feed of posts from users who are feeling the same way, creating a space for empathy, support, and genuine conversation.

Unlike traditional social media platforms, all posts, comments, and conversations are temporary and automatically expire, leaving no permanent digital footprint.

---

## Features

### 🎭 Mood-Based Feed

* Select your current mood when logging in.
* Provide context for how you're feeling.
* View posts from users sharing the same mood.

### 🔒 Privacy First

* No permanent posts.
* No permanent comments.
* Temporary interactions that disappear after a set period.
* Designed to minimize users' digital footprint.

### ❤️ Meaningful Interactions

* Create temporary posts.
* React to posts.
* Leave comments.
* Engage with people experiencing similar emotions.

### 🌱 Authentic Social Experience

Mood Social encourages honest expression by removing the pressure of building a permanent online identity. Every session starts fresh, allowing users to share openly without worrying about long-term consequences.

---

## How It Works

1. Sign in.
2. Select your current mood.
3. Share why you're feeling that way.
4. Browse posts from people experiencing the same mood.
5. Create posts, react, and comment.
6. Posts and conversations automatically expire after a set time.

---

## Why Mood Social?

Traditional social media often prioritizes popularity, engagement metrics, and permanent content.

Mood Social focuses on:

* Privacy
* Authenticity
* Emotional connection
* Temporary conversations
* Shared experiences

The goal is to create a healthier online community where users feel comfortable expressing themselves without leaving a lasting digital trail.
